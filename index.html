Sukhoi Su-57 - Russian Stealth Fighter

Overview

The Su-57 is a fifth-generation stealth multirole fighter aircraft, designed for air superiority and attack missions. It combines advanced avionics, stealth technology, and super-maneuverability.

Radar & Sensors

Equipped with advanced AESA radar capable of detecting targets at long distances. Integrated sensor suite enables simultaneous air and ground target tracking.

Physical Specifications

Length: 20.1 m
Wingspan: 14 m
Height: 4.7 m
Max Speed: 2,600 km/h
Service Ceiling: 20,000 m
Combat Range: 1,500 km

Skills & Capabilities

  • Stealth Technology
  • Supermaneuverability
  • Advanced Avionics
  • Long-Range Target Detection
  • Multirole Combat
  • Air-to-Air & Air-to-Ground Missiles
